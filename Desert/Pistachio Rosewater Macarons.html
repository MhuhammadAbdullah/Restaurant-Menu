Pistachio Rosewater Macarons

Recipe Summary: Delicate meringue-based cookies sandwiched with a fragrant pistachio and rosewater buttercream, topped with chopped pistachios.

Recipe Description:

Experience the exotic flavors of the Middle East with our Pistachio Rosewater Macarons recipe!
These delicate French meringue-based cookies are sandwiched with a fragrant and creamy buttercream,
infused with the nutty flavor of pistachios and the sweetness of rosewater, creating a truly unique and delicious dessert.
