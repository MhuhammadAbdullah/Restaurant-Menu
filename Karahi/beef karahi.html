Beef Karahi

Recipe Summary: A hearty and flavorful curry, Beef Karahi is made with tender beef strips, crispy onions, bell peppers, and a blend of spices in a rich tomato-based sauce.

Recipe Description:

Succulent beef strips cooked in a flavorful and spicy curry sauce with crispy onions, bell peppers, and a blen of spices. Serve with naan or rice for a satisfying meal.
